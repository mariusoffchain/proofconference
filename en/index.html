Online Privacy
talk +workshop Proof of Privacy ~90 min
Why privacy is a fundamental right - online and in real life. Encryption, secure messaging, VPNs and Tor, and the everyday tools that keep your data yours.

Networks & Self-Hosting
talk +workshop Proof of Autonomy ~90 min
Home servers, self-hosted AI, home cloud alternatives, and Nostr - the decentralized social network - practical tools for taking your infrastructure back from Big Tech. The thread continues: from privacy to full sovereignty over your data and tools.

Monetary Sovereignty
talk +workshop Who Controls Your Money ~60 min
Central bank centralization, commercial banks, the digital euro, and the financial legislation reaching far beyond crypto - into every app that touches your money. A closer look at Bitcoin too: not a replacement, but a complementary alternative worth understanding.
